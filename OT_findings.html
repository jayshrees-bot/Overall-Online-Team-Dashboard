📋 PRESALES FINDINGS · 268 Audits
🔴 Personalisation Gap Soft Skills 183 errors
Personalisation is the single biggest gap across ALL presales channels — WhatsApp (126 audits), Inbound (51), Outbound (33), OMNI (20), and Clive (38). JCs are not addressing the customer by name, not referencing previous interaction history, and not tailoring their pitch. Comments like 'no personalisation', 'no empathy', 'no probing at all' appear across all sub-teams.
🔴 Follow-up Not Done Follow Up 19 errors
JCs are promising to call back, share links, or provide price updates — and not following through. 19 recorded instances of missed follow-up. This is especially visible in Clive and WhatsApp where JCs commit to 'will get back in 24 hrs' and cx returns 2 days later without any update.
🟡 Sales Closure Missed Sales Closure 8 errors
JCs are not converting interest to purchase intent. CSAT is not being pitched, lead not created in Oneview, and no verbal commitment taken from the customer to buy. This is especially seen on 'Jewellery Selection' and 'Wants to know Offers' queries where JC solves the query but doesn't close.
🟡 Probing Insufficient Probing 21 errors
JCs are not probing to understand buying intent — budget, occasion, preference, or urgency. Especially visible on Jewellery Selection (51 audits) and EMI queries where understanding the cx's financial preference before recommending would significantly improve conversion rate.
🟢 Call Closing Incomplete Call closing 16 errors
16 audits ended without proper closure — no farewell, no CSAT, no further assistance check. JCs are rushing to end the interaction especially on WhatsApp and Outbound channels. A proper closing increases cx satisfaction and reopens the conversation loop.
📞 POSTSALES FINDINGS · 561 Audits
🔴 Empathy Consistently Missing Soft Skills 197+ errors
Failed to portray empathy is the single largest error in the postsales dataset. JCs are not acknowledging customer frustration — especially on Order Status (112 audits), Cancellation (77 audits), and Failed Delivery (22 audits) queries where customers are already emotionally charged. JCs jump straight to process information without owning the cx's frustration first.
🔴 Ownership Not Taken Solution 30 errors
30 instances of JCs failing to take ownership of the resolution. JCs are saying 'I will check and let you know' without committing to a clear timeline, or are escalating unnecessarily to other teams when the resolution was within their purview. Cx ends calls without a confirmed next step.
🔴 Incorrect Information Given Solution 26 errors
26 audits flagged for incorrect TATs, wrong status updates, and inaccurate process information. This directly causes repeat contacts and NCFs. Most prevalent on Repair Charges (47 audits), Refund Status, and Cancellation queries where process knowledge gaps are most visible.
🟡 Retention Not Attempted Solution 21 errors
On 15 Days Moneyback (22 audits) and Cancellation (77 audits) queries, JCs are processing returns without making genuine retention attempts. Cx's willingness to exchange or accept alternatives is not being tested before the return is processed. Direct revenue impact.
🟡 Oneview Not Checked Probing 21 errors
21 instances of JCs not checking previous interaction history before engaging the customer. This forces cx to repeat themselves, increases handle time, and leads to duplicate efforts. Most visible in Postsales Inbound and WhatsApp channels where cx contacts multiple times on the same issue.
🟡 Incorrect Disposition Tagging Tagging 18 errors
18 audits with incorrect 2nd level dispositions selected in Ameyo/Freshchat. JCs are tagging based on secondary queries rather than the primary reason for contact. This affects reporting accuracy and makes it harder to identify repeat contacts.
🟢 Tone Not Inviting Soft Skills 35 errors
35 instances of tone being flagged as 'not inviting' — JCs sounding flat, robotic, or rushing. This is separate from empathy and refers to overall engagement quality. Especially visible on Call/Chat Etiquette parameter across Postsales Inbound. JCs should sound warm and confident throughout.
Recommended Actions
Priority Action Plan
Based on frequency, severity, and customer impact.
📋 Presales — This Week
1. Personalisation Script — Add customer name usage + context check as mandatory step at interaction start. Make it part of the greeting checklist.
2. Follow-up Tracker — JCs must log promised callbacks in Oneview. Auditor to spot-check if the callback was made within committed TAT.
3. Closure Checklist — CSAT pitch, check for further queries, and farewell must be documented in every interaction.
📞 Postsales — This Week
1. Empathy Framework — Train JCs on 3-step empathy response: Acknowledge → Apologise → Act. Script must include empathy phrase before any process information is shared.
2. Oneview Check SOP — Make Oneview previous interaction check a mandatory first step before engaging cx. Auditors to flag every miss.
3. Retention Training — Refresh retention techniques for Moneyback and Cancellation queries. JC must attempt at least one exchange or value pitch before processing return.
